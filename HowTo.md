# BELA "How To"

## Search

When searching for diagrams or elements, type a fragment of their name and type.

Typing: `Person`
Will find:
  - `getPerson() [method]`
  - `PersonController [class]`
  - `PersonImpl [class]`

you can use `*` to represent any part of the name and type.

Will find:
  - `PersonController [class]`
  - `PersonImpl [class]`
