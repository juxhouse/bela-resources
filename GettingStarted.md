# Getting Started with BELA

#### If Your Company Already Uses BELA

- Get in touch with the BELA admins in your company to get access to BELA.
- Get a good grasp of the fundamental [BELA Concepts](Concepts.md).
- Optional: [Analyse a sofware project](CodeSynchronization.md) and take a look at the generated architecture file that will be sent to BELA.

#### If You Have Subscribed to BELA

 - Get a good grasp of the fundamental [BELA Concepts](Concepts.md).
 - Optional: [Analyse a sofware project](CodeSynchronization.md) and take a look at the generated architecture file that will be sent to BELA.
 - [Schedule an onboarding call](https://calendar.app.google/C5y4SqGjAE8CDKuz5) at your earliest convenience.

#### If You are Starting a Free Trial

 - Visit the [BELA homepage](https://bela.live).
 - Get a good grasp of the fundamental [BELA Concepts](Concepts.md).
 - Optional: [Analyse a sofware project](CodeSynchronization.md) and take a look at the generated architecture file that will be sent to BELA.
 - [Schedule an onboarding call](https://calendar.app.google/C5y4SqGjAE8CDKuz5) at your earliest convenience.
